## Telecom Churn Analysis — Key Insights & Recommendations

### Insight 1: Contract Type is Strongest Churn Predictor
Month-to-month customers churn at 42% vs 11% for one-year
and 3% for two-year contracts.
**Implication:** Contract conversion campaigns = top priority.

### Insight 2: Revenue at Risk is $8M
1,869 churned customers = $8.2M in lost customer lifetime value.
High-risk segment accounts for majority of this loss.

### Insight 3: Service Quality is #1 Controllable Driver
Attitude of support staff (192 churns) is top reason.
Staff training could outperform price reductions.

### Insight 4: New Customers Churn Most
0-12 month customers have highest churn rate.
Onboarding programs needed urgently.

### Insight 5: Geographic Churn Hotspots Exist
Certain California cities show very high churn rates.
Localized network or competitor issues likely.

---

### Recommendation 1: Contract Conversion Campaign
Target high-CLTV month-to-month customers with
15% first-year discount for annual plans.

### Recommendation 2: Tech Support Bundling
Customers with Tech Support churn at half the rate.
Bundle it free for Fiber optic users for 6 months.

### Recommendation 3: Proactive Retention Outreach
Top 20 customers: high CLTV + high Churn Score.
Personal account manager calls within 30 days.
Estimated CLTV at stake: $100,000+
